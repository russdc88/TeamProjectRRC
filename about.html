About Soda Guru


Mixed soda is a growing trend in utah with places like thirst and swig placed all over the state. But what if you cant get to one of these places or you aren't near you, well thats the goal of Soda Guru.

Soda Guru is a compiled list of utah gas stations and the syrups each one holds this allows you to go to your local gas station and mix your own soda the way you want. We also allow you to help you fellow soda head by going to our syrup page and filling out the form to update our database with the amount of syrup at you gas station.
