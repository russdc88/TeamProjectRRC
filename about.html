About Soda Guru


Mixed soda is a growing trend in Utah and "restaurants" like Thirst, Swig, and Sodalicious are sprouting up all over the state. But have you ever been in a situation when one of those pesky soda cravings suddenly descends upon you and Sodalicious, et al., are MILES AWAY? Well, that's why we invented Soda Guru.

Soda Guru compiles a list of the nearest gas stations from your current location, maps them out for your convenience, displays the flavors available at each station and finally, includes the quantity of syrups the stations currently have available. All you have to do is drive, run or speed-walk to the gas station of your choice, fill up the largest cup you can find with your favorite soda, and add the syrups of your choice to your heart's content.

But don't forget to update how much syrup is left so that all soda-syrup-lovers in the area can stay up to date with what the gas station has in stock.
